ICS to GCal — Privacy Policy

“ICS to GCal” will

  • fetch and display a list of your Google calendars;
  • read the content of *.ics files if you use the “Add to calendar” entry in the context menu of a link to such a file;
  • create or update events in your Google calendars using the information obtained by parsing these files.
The parsing happens locally in your browser using ical.js. Data is only transferred via HTTPS connections and only as required by the Google Calendar API.
